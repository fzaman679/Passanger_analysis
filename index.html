Introduction

United States is known as the country of opportunities. Millions travel to the US from all over the globe, for tourism, work, visit family and friends. Domestically US citizens frequently travel for work or leisure.

Analysis

The number of passengers varies based upon employment rate. International travellers arrive to the US from countries where Visas are easily obtained.

Domestic Travel

Employment Rate

In this graph, we can see as the unemployment rate decreased from 2018 to 2019 by 21%. This caused the number of passengers to increase. With increase cashflow led more travel within the nation. From 2019 to 2020 the number of passengers have decreased as the unemployment rate increased, due to the pandemic.

Pandemic

The Pandemic caused the economy to drop dramatically. In March 2020, the number of passengers have dropped as the unemployment rate has skyrocketed. However, after March, as the unemployment rate decreased, the number of passengers increased.

International Travel

Asylum Seekers

Majority of asylum seekers hold Mexican passports as well as the number of passengers arrive from Mexico. People from Guatemala and Honduras travel through Mexico to seek asylum in the United States of America.

Wisa Waiver

Majority of the countries where most of the passengers arrive are from Visa Waiver countries. That is because citizens of those countries do not need a visa in order to arrive to USA. Same applies to Canada and Mexico.

Generally Speaking, the unemployment rate and visa issuance are the key players in travel, foreign and domestic. The pandemic was a huge hit for the whole economy. Increased employment rates increases travel.
